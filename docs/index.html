How It Works

1

Launch Slowdown

The app lives in your menu bar with a floating overlay showing your current WPM.

2

Select Audio Source

Choose between your microphone or system audio to capture speech from any app.

3

Start Monitoring

Click play and start speaking. Watch your pace in real-time and adjust as needed.

4

Improve Over Time

With practice, you'll naturally learn to maintain an optimal speaking pace.

System Requirements

Installation

1

Download & Open DMG

Download Slowdown.dmg and double-click to open it.

2

Drag to Applications

Drag Slowdown.app to your Applications folder.

3

First Launch

Important: Right-click (or Control-click) on Slowdown and select "Open". This is required because the app is not signed with an Apple Developer certificate. You only need to do this once.

4

Grant Permissions

Allow microphone access and speech recognition when prompted. All processing happens on-device.
